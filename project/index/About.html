About Our Restaurant

This modest hotel in a mixed residential and commercial area is 1 kilometer from Central Park, and 2 kilometer from both the long-standing Kos Minars stone markers and Ambala City train station...

The casual rooms come with Wi-Fi (free) and flat-screen TVs, plus minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Room service is available 24/7.

Amenities include an informal restaurant and a bar.
